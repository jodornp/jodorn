a bit about me

  • byAdmin
  • Posted23 August 2021

Welcome to my personal website. My name is Jodorn. I enjoy drawing manga and comic books, building websites, photography, watching movies and anime, and reading the Bible.

One of my favorite spots is the beach. There's nothing quite like spending a day at the beach to unwind. The scent of the ocean, the sound of waves crashing onto the shore, and the sensation of sand beneath my feet are all incredibly soothing. Swimming and surfing in the ocean always make for the best days I've ever had.

I really enjoy keeping fish. I have a 65-gallon aquarium with African Cichlids, mostly Demasoni. I breed and raise them, and it makes me happy to watch them grow. :-)

Drawing - manga & comics

I have a strong enthusiasm for drawing, particularly comics and manga. There are times when I draw without a clear idea of what I’m creating, but I just go for it. I've been pursuing this hobby of drawing since 1992. I enjoy sketching and doodling as well. This love for art is what motivates me. It’s this passion that drives both me and others who share it to grab our pencils and put them on paper. See my PORTFOLIO
